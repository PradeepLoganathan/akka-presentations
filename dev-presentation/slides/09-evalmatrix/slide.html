Definition

An EvalMatrix is the system's governance posture as source.

Row fieldQuestion it answers
ControlWhat obligation, risk, or business rule applies?
MeasurementWhat must be scored, labeled, or observed?
ConsequenceIs it blocking, non-blocking, advisory, or build-gating?
Enforcement locusRuntime, HITL, HOTL, CI/CD, deployment, timer, event, evidence.
ProofWhat record proves the control operated?
