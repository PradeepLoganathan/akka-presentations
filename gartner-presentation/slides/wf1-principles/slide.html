Workforce Adoption

Scaling AI starts with principles.

Before scaling tools, scale judgment.

  1. 01Use data and AI aligned to our Code of Business Conduct and Trust Center policies.
  2. 02Prioritize customer, colleague, and organizational safety through governance-first practices.
  3. 03Assume AI tools become the primary interface for employees, partners, and customers.
  4. 04Enable employee and partner agency to take risks, move faster, and build skill.
  5. 05Create transparency and acceptance as roles become more fluid.
  6. 06Learn from the controlled injuries that emerge from autonomy.
  7. 07Build an AI-first competency we can bring to the market.
  8. 08Invest in enablement and training — fluency is a skill, and judgment under AI must be taught, not assumed. It’s how we get the most from these tools and avoid laziness and fatigue.
