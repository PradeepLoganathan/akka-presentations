Adoption Reality

AI makes people faster.
It can also make them slower.

The adoption challenge is preserving judgment under acceleration.

Leverage
  • Faster synthesis
  • Pattern detection
  • Ambiguity compression
  • Draft generation

Productivity gains are real when AI is applied to the right work.

Friction
  • AI laziness — polished output, light review
  • AI fatigue — more prompting and checking
  • Review debt — savings spent verifying intent
  • Workslop — complete-looking, low-value output
AI does not remove judgment. It raises the premium on judgment.
