The challenge with agentic AI
Agentic AI amplifies production complexity.
To ship an agentic agent, teams must deliver dozens of components — and stitching them together evenly is on you.
Intent
  • What is its purpose?
  • Who is accountable?
  • Where does it operate?
Autonomy
  • What decisions can it influence?
  • How autonomous may it be?
  • When must humans intervene?
Boundaries
  • Who can be affected?
  • What data may it use?
  • What dependencies shape it?
Posture
  • What risk tier applies?
  • What evidence must it produce?
  • How is it evaluated?
