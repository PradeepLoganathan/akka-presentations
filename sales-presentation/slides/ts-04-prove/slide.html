How it works · 02
Production traffic trains your model and then tested in staging.
An open-weights base (4–12B) is tuned with reinforcement learning against your interaction data — your evaluations are the grader. Training runs on GPUs in your cloud account: in-VPC, sovereign, air-gapped where required. The result is a specialized SLM — a ~50MB adapter. You own the weights.
Every promotion ships with an auto-generated before/after benchmark — computed by your evaluations, on your traffic, stored on the audit record.
After promotion, the loop keeps grading. Drift in traffic or upstream models registers in eval scores first; retraining triggers automatically; a new candidate re-enters staging. Quality below the bar triggers instant rollback to the incumbent.
The loop runs inside your Improvement Policy: what it optimizes toward, how far it may tune, what it may never touch. Every decision lands on the immutable interaction record.
