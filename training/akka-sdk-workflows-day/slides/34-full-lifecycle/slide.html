Putting it together

One order, all the moving parts.

1 · HTTPPOST /api/orders arrives at OrderEndpoint
2 · WorkflowOrderWorkflow.start; places order; schedules abandon timer
3 · ReserveInventoryEntity.reserve; OrderEntity.markReserved
4 · ChargePaymentEntity.charge; OrderEntity.markPaid; timer cancelled
5 · ShipOrderEntity.markShipped; workflow ends
6 · View + auditOrdersView updated; audit consumer picks up any customer events
Each numbered box could span multiple JVMs across a cluster. You wrote none of that.
