Testing & ops

The console is your friend.

localhost:9000/akka/console shows every workflow instance, its current step, its state, and its scheduled timers.

Workflows tab

See in-flight workflows. Click into one to inspect state, current step, transition history.

Timers tab

All scheduled timers with delay, target, and name. Delete or trigger manually while debugging.

Entities tab

Any entity by id - state and event log. See exactly what the workflow persisted.

Nothing has to be right the first time. The console tells you what actually happened, event by event.
